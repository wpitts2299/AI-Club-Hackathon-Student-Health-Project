Therapist login options:
- Default CSV path: data/therapists.csv (override with HACKATHON_THERAPIST_CSV).
- Accepts either:
  - Username/password from CSV (creates a cookie session), or
  - X-Therapist-Key header/query param matching HACKATHON_THERAPIST_KEY or HACKATHON_API_KEY.
- Login form at /therapist requires one of the above. If the CSV is missing, add records in the format:
    therapist_name,therapist_id,username,password
    Demo Therapist,0001,therapist,changeme
